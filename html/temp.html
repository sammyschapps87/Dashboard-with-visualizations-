Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the sourthern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of t he earth at the time of the year the data was gathered.
